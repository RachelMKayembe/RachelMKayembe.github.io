About The Kays Beauty

Welcome to The Kay’s Beauty Salon, an African hair salon nestled in the heart of Ottawa, EST. Since our establishment in early 2023, our mission has been to empower individuals to embrace the natural beauty of their hair, with a particular focus on braids and dreads

At The Kay’s Beauty Salon, we recognize that hair is not just an accessory but a powerful form of self-expression. Our talented and passionate team is dedicated to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where clients can feel inspired and celebrated. With their expertise and enthusiasm, they offer incredible hair treatments that go beyond mere styling.
